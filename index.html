Vehicle Guidelines

Once you've got your uniforms sorted, it's time to select the vehicle you wish to drive.

Vehicles are available via the F2 Menu. Press F2, then select Police. The vehicle types are:

  • Marked – Police vehicles with liveries/signwriting
  • Unmarked – Police vehicles that have no liveries/signwriting
  • Specialist – Vehicles used by certain workgroups
  • Unlockable – Vehicles available for purchase on the BHRP Store

Select your vehicle from the list and spawn it (ensuring your uniform matches the vehicle).

Vehicle Model (Colour Detail) Category
Vehicle Modifications

Police vehicles are only allowed certain modifications. All vehicles can be assumed to allow white unless otherwise stated.

If a vehicle is not listed, and does not have a livery that overwrites the colour, only white can be used.

Secondary colours are open for discussion if they remain appropriate.

Vehicle Classes

Vehicle Category Outline

  • Category A Vehicles: Must not be used for pursuits if heavily laden; must be fitted with emergency warning devices.
  • Category B Vehicles: Must not be used for pursuits if heavily laden; a pursuit controller must replace this vehicle as soon as possible; must be fitted with emergency warning devices.
  • Category C Vehicles: Can be used for pursuits if no other vehicle is available and not heavily laden; a pursuit controller must replace this vehicle with a Category A vehicle as soon as possible; must be fitted with emergency warning devices.
  • Category D Vehicles: Heavy 4WDs in remote areas may be used for pursuits or urgent duty driving if no other vehicle is available and if they do not exceed the open road speed limit; a pursuit controller must replace this vehicle as soon as possible; must be fitted with emergency warning devices.

Uniform Guidelines

Once you've set up your character appearance, you can get the Police Uniforms you wish to wear.

Press T to open your chat window and type /eup.

This will bring up the uniform menu where you can choose your uniforms.

Set your character with the uniform items you wish; keep it appropriate and realistic.

Once you have your uniforms:

  • Press F1
  • Select Player Related Options
  • Select Player Appearance
  • Select Save Ped

You have now saved your Police Uniforms and can respawn them next time via the Saved Peds menu.

You can change uniform items anytime through the F1 menu or via /eup.

Uniform Setup Instructions
  • Press T and type /eup to access the uniform menu.
  • Choose your desired uniform items.
  • Press F1 → Player Related Options → Player Appearance → Save Ped.
